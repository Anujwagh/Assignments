The Impact of Social Media on Society

In the last two decades, social media has dramatically transformed the way people communicate, share information, and perceive the world around them. Platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter (now X), TikTok, and others have become deeply integrated into everyday life. While social media offers many benefits, such as connecting people globally and providing platforms for self-expression and activism, it also presents significant challenges related to mental health, misinformation, and privacy. As such, understanding both the positive and negative effects of social media is essential for navigating the modern digital age.
